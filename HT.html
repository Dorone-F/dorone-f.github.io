Health & Technology

Presentation of the Major

The Health and Technology Major aims to present the applications of engineering training in trades directly or indirectly related to health.
It provides medical knowledge and the vocabulary necessary for communication between the engineer and the healthcare professional.
It helps to understand the complexity of national and international health systems, both in terms of the payment of health costs and the exchange of information, regulations, security and standards applicable to information systems as well as to connected objects which invade the daily lives of patients and will play a major role in their monitoring tomorrow.
Habits are changing and engineers are at the heart of the technological revolution.
It is up to them to understand the issues and define what will be the health of tomorrow.

Objectifs of the Major

  • Present the applications of engineering training in trades directly or indirectly related to health.
  • To put the engineer in a situation to better understand the disease or disability and put the end user at the center of the designer's concerns.
  • Understand the complexity of health systems at the national and international level, both in terms of the coverage of health costs and the exchange of information, regulation, security and standards applicable to information systems as well as connected objects which invade the daily lives of patients today and will play a major role in their monitoring tomorrow.
