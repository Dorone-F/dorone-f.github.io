Displacement system for park care staff Zoological of Paris

Project presentation

The Paris Zoological Park wishes to improve and facilitate the movement of its teams caregivers within the park, for this, the establishment of a fleet of electric scooters destination of nursing staff will be implemented.

  • The objectives of electric scooters are to:
    • Decrease the fatigue of caregivers
    • Facilitate the rapid response to animals
    • Increase the public's perception of the presence of staff
    • Reduce operating costs by increasing surveillance rounds

  • The movement of electric scooters will be done without:
    • noise
    • CO2 emission

Project specification

  • In order to fulfill the Zoo's request, technical requirements are imposed:
    • Quickly transport a member of the nursing staff in any point of the Park,
    • Perform rounds in less than 30 minutes,
    • Perform at least 2 laps without recharging,
    • 3 recharging stations,
    • Securing of electric scooters using a name badge,
    • Geolocation and autonomy of the scooters transmit to a control,
    • Recharge in less than 4 hours, each scooter in 3 points of the Park.

  • Technical constraints:
    • Distribution of scooters at 3 locations in the Park:
      • Information post at the entrance to the Park
      • Veterinary clinic
      • Big Rock
